What is the purpose of this role?

We’re hiring a Manager, SEO & AEO to lead the strategy and execution of HelloTech’s organic growth, AI visibility, and website conversion performance. You’ll own the roadmap for search and answer engine optimization while also ensuring our site experience converts traffic into loyal, high-LTV customers.


What is the role?

This is a cross-functional role at the intersection of SEO, content, AI, and performance design. You’ll lead everything from search rankings and site health to landing page testing and CRO strategies. Your work will drive measurable results across D2C and B2B.


Who is DOOR?

DOOR (formerly Latch) is unlocking the next era of Building Intelligence. We combine premium hardware, intuitive software, and automated operations into one seamless platform—helping multifamily properties run more efficiently, grow revenue, and deliver smarter, more connected living experiences. From smart access and in-unit automation to building-wide controls, DOOR empowers owners, operators, and property teams to reduce overhead, protect asset value, and stand out in a competitive market.


We believe smarter buildings make life simpler. At DOOR, you’ll help shape a future where buildings anticipate needs, operate effortlessly, and quietly improve life for everyone inside.

About HelloTech
HelloTech, a DOOR company, is redefining in-home tech services. As the leading national provider of on-demand smart home installation and tech support, we deliver professional, affordable, and seamless service to consumers and property managers alike—anytime, anywhere.


We’re building a search and conversion engine that ranks across Google and AI platforms, and turns traffic into booked services. That’s where you come in.


What will you do?

Lead the full strategy and execution of SEO and AEO programs to drive organic visibility, traffic, and engagement across traditional search and AI-powered platforms
Optimize HelloTech’s website performance and conversion funnel, identifying opportunities to improve UX, reduce bounce rate, and increase booking and lead conversions
Build and scale high-impact content that ranks in Google, appears in featured snippets, and is favored by LLMs like ChatGPT and Gemini
Partner with design and engineering to execute conversion rate optimization (CRO) tests, including A/B experiments on landing pages, CTAs, copy, and layouts
Manage technical SEO health including site speed, mobile performance, structured data, crawlability, and Core Web Vitals
Use AI tools to accelerate content strategy, keyword clustering, and site audits
Establish reporting frameworks that track organic traffic, keyword performance, site engagement, conversion rates, and revenue impact
Collaborate across Marketing, Product, and Engineering to align on-site structure and campaigns with SEO and CRO best practices, leveraging Webflow’s CMS and visual design tools
Act as the internal champion for SEO, AEO, and CRO best practices, helping the company stay ahead of evolving algorithms and AI search behavior
Identify future team needs and help scale SEO and CRO functions as HelloTech and DOOR grow

What do you bring to DOOR?

3 to 5+ years of hands-on SEO experience with proven results in both D2C and B2B environments. 1+ years of AEO experience.
Demonstrated ability to lead both strategy and execution across SEO, AEO, and CRO initiatives
Experience working within Webflow or similar visual CMS platforms, with a clear understanding of their SEO capabilities and constraints
Deep technical SEO knowledge including crawl optimization, Core Web Vitals, schema, indexing, and JavaScript rendering
Strong experience with conversion rate optimization, website analytics, and A/B testing tools (e.g. Google Optimize, VWO, Optimizely)
Expertise in Answer Engine Optimization, with an understanding of how to rank in AI-powered search experiences
High comfort level using AI tools to streamline workflows and optimize content at scale
Proficiency with Google Search Console, GA4, SEMrush, Ahrefs, and CMS environments
Strong understanding of content frameworks that balance search demand, UX, and conversion intent
Analytical mindset with the ability to report on performance and recommend data-driven optimizations
Strong communicator who collaborates effectively across content, design, product, and engineering

Attention Job Seekers: Beware of job scams!

Job scams are on the rise, and you should take steps to protect yourself in your job search. Here are some guidelines to help you stay safe if you are applying for a job with Latch or DOOR:

Communications from Latch or DOOR will always come from @latch.com or @door.com. Do not accept a job interview invitation with Latch or DOOR from anyone who does not contact you through an email that comes from @latch.com or @door.com.
Latch never interviews candidates via Skype or in writing where you do not see the interviewer. All of our interviews happen either by video or in person.
We will never offer you a job based on a single conversation with someone claiming to be from our People organization. In all of our job interviews, you will always speak, by video or in person, with the hiring manager for the position.
Do not share your sensitive personal information with anyone who purports to offer you a job at Latch or DOOR unless you verified that the offer is from Latch or DOOR (check email senders addresses). We will never ask you for your bank account information or your mother’s maiden name during the interview process.
We will never ask you to purchase any equipment with a promise that we will reimburse you after you begin working for us.
If you believe you were a victim of a job scam, you may report it to the FBI: https://www.ic3.gov/.
