Job Title:

SEO Manager

Experience Required:

Minimum 4+ years of experience in SEO and digital marketing

Location: Vesu, Surat, Gujarat / Remote

Job Type:

Full-Time

Job Summary:

We are looking for a highly skilled and strategic SEO Manager with 4+ years of hands-on experience to lead and scale our organic growth initiatives. The ideal candidate will be responsible for developing SEO strategies, managing execution across teams, improving search visibility, and driving consistent organic traffic and conversions.

Key Responsibilities:

Develop, own, and execute comprehensive SEO strategies aligned with business goals
Manage and optimize on-page, off-page, and technical SEO initiatives
Lead keyword research, competitor analysis, and opportunity mapping
Oversee technical SEO audits including crawlability, indexing, site speed, and Core Web Vitals
Plan and manage link-building strategies and outreach campaigns
Collaborate with content, design, and development teams to ensure SEO best practices
Review and optimize website architecture, internal linking, and metadata
Track SEO performance, rankings, traffic, and conversions; prepare detailed reports
Mentor and guide junior SEO executives or team members
Required Skills & Qualifications:

4+ years of proven experience in SEO and digital marketing
Strong expertise in technical SEO, on-page optimization, and content SEO
Hands-on experience with SEO tools: Google Search Console, Google Analytics (GA4), Ahrefs, SEMrush
Good understanding of HTML, website architecture, and CMS platforms
Proven experience in backlink building, outreach, and authority growth
Strong analytical, problem-solving, and decision-making skills
Ability to translate data into actionable SEO insights
Excellent English communication skills (written and verbal)
Preferred Qualifications:

Experience managing SEO teams or projects
Strong experience with WordPress or other CMS platforms
Knowledge of content marketing and SEO-driven content strategies
Basic understanding of Google Ads / PPC and how SEO integrates with paid marketing
Understanding of UX, CRO, and user intent optimization
Benefits (Optional):

Competitive salary
Leadership and career growth opportunities
Paid SEO tools and training
Friendly and growth-oriented work environment
